Peruvian Artists

Explore the voices and visions of Peru through its visual artists, musicians, and performers—each reflecting the country’s soul and cultural richness.

Visual Artists

Tilsa Tsuchiya

A surrealist painter who explored Peruvian mythology and feminine identity in her symbolic works.

Fernando de Szyszlo

One of Peru’s most influential modern artists, blending abstraction with Andean spirituality.

Bruno Portuguez

A vibrant Amazonian painter known for his colorful, energetic art celebrating nature and heritage.

Nicario Jiménez Quispe

known for his vibrant, hand-carved altarpieces that blend Andean tradition with contemporary themes.s.

Music & Performing Arts

Susana Baca

An Afro-Peruvian icon whose music has preserved cultural roots and inspired generations worldwide.

Yma Súmac

Legendary soprano with an unmatched vocal range who introduced Andean sounds to the global stage.

Vania Masías

Contemporary dancer and choreographer blending traditional Peruvian steps with modern movement.

Saywa

known for her powerful voice and for preserving Andean music and indigenous identity through her performances..
